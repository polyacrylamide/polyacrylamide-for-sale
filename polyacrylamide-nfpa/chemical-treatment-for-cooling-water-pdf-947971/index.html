Chemical treatments such as scale inhibitors prevent scaling by dispersing the minerals and preventing them from forming deposits. Suppresses Microbiological Growth: The warm and humid environment of cooling water systems is an ideal breeding ground for microorganisms, which can cause fouling and biofilm formation. Chemical treatments such as biocides are used to control the growth of these microorganisms and prevent biofilm formation.
